Welcome to the Sarbajna Lab

Designing next-generation sustainable organometallic catalysts for a greener future.

Our Mission

  • Creating environmentally friendly and atom-economical catalytic systems.
  • Elucidating reaction mechanisms to guide rational catalyst design.
  • Addressing global challenges in green and sustainable chemistry.

What We Do

  • Organometallic chemistry and homogeneous catalysis.
  • Activation of small molecules (H₂, N₂, CO₂, etc.).
  • Design and synthesis of multifunctional ligands.
  • Mechanistic and computational studies to uncover catalytic pathways.

Why It Matters

By integrating metal–ligand cooperation with green chemistry principles, our research paves the way for innovative solutions in energy, materials science, and sustainable chemical manufacturing.
